About

Practical experience, local roots, and a focus on making work easier.

Palmetto Business Automation exists to help small businesses reduce the friction that slows their teams down. I work on the practical parts of the workflow: Excel cleanup, report repair, dashboard views, automation, and simple systems that people can actually maintain.

My background includes finance, business management, analysis, and process improvement across aerospace, construction, DoD, industrial maintenance, and contracts environments. I enjoy turning repetitive work into something clearer and easier to run.

I am a retired United States Air Force veteran, a Lowcountry professional, and a graduate of the College of Charleston and Charleston Southern University. I also hold a Lean Six Sigma certificate from Villanova University and a Post Graduate Program in Data Analytics from Purdue University.

Why I started this

Helping small businesses turn repetitive work into simpler systems.

The goal is simple: make reporting clearer, reduce manual effort, and give small businesses practical automation that fits the way they already work.
